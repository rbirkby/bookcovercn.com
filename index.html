Website Categorisation for Bluecoat Proxies

Websites can be classified using the online form available at https://sitereview.bluecoat.com/#/. This is a very quick process, often taking under 1 hour and will be synchronised with enterprise proxy servers automatically.

Enterprise employees should be encouraged to categorise websites instead of using alternative means of viewing the website.
